“RESEARCH GOALS & OBJECTIVES”
I set the following research goals to serve as a foundation to guide the
rest of my design. I surveyed 5 people and conducted three in-depth
interviews based on the following goals:
-To better understand user behavior around the activity of Art Therapy.

-To determine which tasks users would like to complete using an Art Therapy Expert app.

-Documenting user pain points with existing Art Therapy Expert Apps on the market.

-Collecting data on the context in which users would use an Art Therapy Expert App.

“RESEARCH FINDINGS : ONLINE SURVEYS (QUANTITATIVE DATA)”
Through conducting an online survey, I was able to get both quantitative data as well as identify
my user’s goals & pain points. The data generated was then used as the basis for my interview
questions. Through this, I was able to determine which direction to take at the early stages of the
app development.
-20% visit a therapist a few times a year.
-60% found user privacy to be the most helpful tool during a therapy session.
-60% would use a therapy app if they showed them locations of the nearest
clinic to go too.
-40% use some form of online therapy / counseling tool.
-60% video chat a few times a week
-100% think that Art Therapy could be a useful technique for counseling.
“USER RESEARCH ANALYSIS”
After analyzing carefully 2 hours of interview material and survey questions, I conducted three
rounds of Affinity Mapping to see what patterns would emerge. This led to a list of helpful
insights that covered topics such as behaviours/attitudes, needs & goals, frustrations,
quotes/facts for the app.

“USER RESEARCH ANALYSIS  (INSIGHTS)”
-The app is free except for added features such as video calling where we will have to

-This is helpful to know as we can identify this as one of the top marketing trends.
-This tells us that perhaps our users would love to gain access to nearby art therapy
  services and not just online.
-One of the best ways to cure mental issues is through social contact as it gives the

“USER PERSONA”
BACKGROUND
The Coolor App is an expert app designed
specifically for Art Therapy. With this app,
patients & Art Therapists will be able to
connect within seconds with one another
through the use of chatting, booked video
sessions & meeting events near them.
We hope to plan a design that is intuitive
enough for both Art Therapists & their clients.
in addition, the app will allow users to pose a
question to any expert, & if they feel they are
the best to answer it, they can schedule a short
video session with the person asking.
PROBLEM
With the prevalence of counselling services
everywhere, most people still refuse to talk to
a therapist based on a variety of reasons such as
the lack of time, no insurance coverage or the fear
of being judged. This eventually leads to a rise in
mental illness cases.
DESIGN CHALLENGE
How might we design a mobile and web
application that could encourage our users to
try out Art Therapy while gaining their trust
during the whole process?
An Art Therapy app that allows individuals
to have access to a built-in community of
trained and certified Art Therapists as well as
friendly meet up events in one’s area.
